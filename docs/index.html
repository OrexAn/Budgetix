FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. Budgetix works fully offline and stores all data on your device — your finances stay yours.

Can I use multiple currencies?

Yes. Set per-card exchange rates, track results per category, and adjust rates manually when offline.

Is my data private?

Yes. We don’t collect personal data, run ads, or use trackers. See our Privacy Policy.
