The app does not collect or transmit personal data to external servers. All data you enter (such as expenses, income, categories, and notes) is stored locally on your device.

The app does not share your data with third parties. We do not use advertising networks or analytics services that collect user information.

3. Permissions

The app may request access to device storage solely to save and restore your local data. Such data never leaves your device.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, we recommend that you regularly back up your data to avoid accidental loss.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document and are effective immediately upon posting.
